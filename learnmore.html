About Us

Today’s news is overwhelming leaving us very minimal time to catch up on the news that we missed while sleeping. YouNews wants to provide that daily dose of news that we love but

couldn’t catch live. To do so, we will be researching the possible ways to provide this product at a minimal cost.

 

To curate short clips of real-time news, we will use AI models. Unlike other OpenAI forks, though, we will be training our own models to be task-optimized with the help of the recent and

fantastic work of the likes of @Stability AI and others.

 

YouNews isn’t a new CNN or Sky News; quite the contrary. YouNews aggregates and summarizes all information from your favorite news networks to provide a personalized experience.

We will have thorough integration tests with these networks to support as many as possible.

 

YouNews' MVP targets finance enthusiasts globally. Whether you reside in the US and missed the Asian markets or in India and missed out on the American markets, YouFinance will be

there to support you. Once YouFinance has gained traction, we will look into other news topics (YouPolitics, YouSports, and more).

 

While rolling out YouFinance, we want as much valuable feedback as possible from those in the game. We will initially launch the MVP as a private-beta to academics, Wall Street

bankers, and finance students. Their feedback will help us improve our models.
